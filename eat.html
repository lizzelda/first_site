Oxford

Italian

Zizzi's, George Street, City Centre

Zizzi's is a classic Italian chain restaurant. This particular branch has reasonable quality food, but occassionally slow service. Don't go here without a voucher!

Mamma Mia, South Parade, Summertown

Independent restaurant in North Oxford serving tasty, rustic-feel food. Can especially recommend the side salad as having one of the nicest dressings I've come across. Fairly inexpensive, but right at the top of Summertown so transport is required to get from the city centre to this restaurant
